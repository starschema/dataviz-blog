Investing in Data Viz Accessibility Pays Off

In this playful exploration of data visualization accessibility, we delve into why it matters and the steps a person or organization might take on their road to deeper knowledge about the topic.

Reasons to Invest

Legal

Many companies in the world are subject to the legal prohibition of discrimination based on disability. Furthermore, even in lieue of such a law, anti-discrimination lawsuits can still be very costly.

Moral

Caring about disabled users is the right thing to do as it aligns with human rights and the principles of modern democracies. The digital world is currently an inequal space bt we should change that.

Financial

Investing in accessibility expands market size, boosts brand loyalty, enhances product quality, and fosters diversity. Early adoption saves money in the long run.
